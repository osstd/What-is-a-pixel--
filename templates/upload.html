* It's important to clarify that no images are collected or stored on the server during runtime. When you upload an image, the processing occurs in real-time on the server. The processing function extracts RGB values, which are then passed to the function responsible for rendering the results page. The data points derived from the processing are embedded directly in the HTML and used exclusively by your browser to plot the three charts you see. Importantly, once you refresh the page, all data is automatically and permanently erased.
